Features

Design

The drinkware is crafted from BPA free, 18/8 stainless steel. The colorful powder coating on the outside of the bottle is durable and provides a finger print free texture that is easy to clean. The double-walled vacuum insulation prevents condensation from forming on the exterior.

Insulation

All products have double-walled vacuum insulation technology. The space between the stainless steel walls are void of matter, with no medium for heat to transfer. Your drinks temperature will not be affected by the temperature outside the container, staying cold up to 24 hours and hot up to 12 hours.

Warranty

We guarantee every Hydro Flask product against manufacturer's defects for 100 years or one lifetime, whichever comes first.
